Our Mission:

UNICEF Queen's is dedicated to raising awareness and funds for UNICEF's global initiatives.

Founded in 2008, UNICEF Queen's is a campus chapter of UNICEF at Queen's University.
Our purpose is to empower the Queen’s community to make a difference in children's lives.
Our mission is to help the world’s children in need through education, advocacy, and fundraising.
