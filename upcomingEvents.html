Our Mission

UNICEF Queen's is dedicated to raising awareness and funds for UNICEF's global initiatives.

UNICEF, the United Nations International Children’s Emergency Fund is a global, non-profit organization that aims to build a world where the rights of every child are realized. As the largest child-focused humanitarian agency in the world, UNICEF's programs address the full range of children’s rights: Health, Education, Equality, and Protection. Founded in 2008, UNICEF Queen's is a campus chapter of UNICEF at Queen's University. Our purpose is to empower the Queen’s community to make a difference in children's lives. Our mission is to help the world’s children in need through education, advocacy, and fundraising.
